üìê Regulatory Focus: Capital Adequacy

We maintain strict compliance with Basel III, ensuring our **Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR)** remains above regulatory minimums.


$$ \text{Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR)} = \frac{\text{Tier 1 Capital} + \text{Tier 2 Capital}}{\text{Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA)}} $$

**Current Q3 CAR:** $\mathbf{14.5\%}$ ($\text{Regulatory Minimum: } 10.5\%$)

This formula is central to our stability. Our 14.5% CAR reflects a strong capital buffer, well above the required minimum.
